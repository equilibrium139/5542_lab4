WASD- move camera, mouse to look around. Click once so the cursor locks to the canvas. In order to click the buttons, click escape so the cursor reappears. e- scale up, E- scale down, r- rotate person (these only work on the person).

  • 1- rotate neck
  • 2- rotate left upper arm
  • 3- rotate left lower arm
  • 4- rotate right upper arm
  • 5- rotate right lower arm
  • 6- rotate left upper leg
  • 7- rotate left lower leg
  • 8- rotate right upper leg
  • 9- rotate right lower leg
